Events

Running race

Running races are contests to determine which of the competitors are able to run a certain distance in the shortest time.

Relay race

Relay, a track-and-field sport consisting of a set number of stages (legs), each leg run by a different member of a team.

Long Jump

The long jump is a track and field event in which athletes combine speed, strength and agility in an attempt to leap the farthest from a takeoff point.

High Jump

The high jump is a track and field event in which competitors must jump unaided over a horizontal bar placed at measured heights without dislodging it.

Football

Football or soccer, is a team sport played with a spherical ball between two teams of 11 players.

Basketball

Basketball, a game played between two teams of five players each on a rectangular court. Each team tries to score by tossing the ball through the opponent's goal.
